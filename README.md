# Retail Sales & Profitability Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data to identify revenue drivers, loss-making product categories, and the impact of discounting on overall profitability. The goal is to uncover business insights that can support better pricing and operational decisions.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- SQL (Data extraction and aggregation)
- Microsoft Excel (Data exploration and validation)
- Power BI (Data visualization and dashboard creation)

---

## 📊 Analysis Performed
- Revenue analysis by product category and region
- Profitability analysis by sub-category
- Identification of loss-making products
- Discount vs profit relationship analysis
- Customer segment performance evaluation

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Technology category generates the highest overall revenue
- Tables and Bookcases are major loss-making sub-categories
- High discount levels significantly reduce profitability
- Consumer segment contributes the highest sales volume
- Some high-revenue products operate with low profit margins

## 🚀 Business Conclusion
The analysis highlights key revenue drivers and identifies pricing inefficiencies, particularly in high-discount scenarios. Optimizing discount strategies and reviewing loss-making product categories can significantly improve overall profitability.
